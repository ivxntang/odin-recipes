Description

Singapore national delicacy, Chinese-inspired noodles, which were the bulk I came across, are a little less complex but no less fantastic. The smoky kiss of the wok really shines in stir-fried hokkien mee; a superb dish of wonton mee was all about the hoisin-sweet and pickle-sour sauce touched up with just a dab of chile paste and some ridiculous roast pork. Noodle broths are typically pork-, chicken- or shrimp-based.

Ingredients

  • Prawns
  • yellow noodles
  • thick white noodels
  • fried egg
  • squid rings
  • prawn stock

Steps

  1. cook noodles and mix
  2. cook all other ingredients
  3. mix ingredients and stir fry
  4. add stock and stir fry
  5. garnish with lime
